The UrbanNest Journey

Founded in 2020, UrbanNest was born out of a simple idea: that modern furniture should be accessible, sustainable, and built to last. We believe that your home is a reflection of your personality, and every piece of furniture should tell a story of comfort and style.

Our design philosophy is rooted in minimalism. We strip away the unnecessary to focus on what truly matters—quality materials, expert craftsmanship, and timeless aesthetics. From our workshop to your living room, every piece is handled with care.
